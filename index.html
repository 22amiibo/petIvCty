Benefits of the Bill

By enforcing accountability through penalties and legal actions, establishes a dedicated Environmental Justice Division within the DOEE, and prioritizes transparency and public participation in decision-making. Its ultimate goal is to prevent harmful projects from disproportionately impacting overburdened communities unless appropriate steps are taken to mitigate or offset those impacts.
