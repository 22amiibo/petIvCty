Benefits of the Bill
By enforcing accountability through penalties and legal actions, establishes a dedicated Environmental Justice Division within the DOEE, and prioritizes transparency and public participation in decision-making. Its ultimate goal is to prevent harmful projects from disproportionately impacting overburdened communities unless appropriate steps are taken to mitigate or offset those impacts.
Summary
Council of the District of Columbia. Environmental Justice Amendment Act of 2023, B25-0564. Introduced November 6, 2023. https://dccouncil.gov.
Cumulative Impact Assessments Mandates the preparation of a "Cumulative Impact Statement" (CIS) before granting permits for environmentally harmful activities in communities already facing heavy environmental burdens. These statements evaluate the combined environmental, social, and health effects of proposed projects.
